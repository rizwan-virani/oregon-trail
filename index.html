HOW TO PLAY

🎯 The Goal

Get your wagon and your five people from Independence, Missouri to the Willamette Valley in Oregon — about 2,000 miles across 13 legs of trail. Winter is coming behind you. Move too slow and it catches you.

🧭 The Loop

Each leg you travel (days tick by, food burns), hit random events where your choice matters, then face an arcade challenge at the landmark. Forts let you trade and upgrade.

⚔️ Bosses

Five legs end in a real fight: the Kearny bandit gang, a grizzly at Laramie, the South Pass blizzard, a wolf pack near Fort Hall, and the Columbia River rapids. They hit hard. Upgrade first.

🎮 Controls

Shooting: mouse to aim, click or Space to fire, R to reload.
Steering: ← → or A D.
Everything else: click.

🍖 Staying Alive

Food feeds people. Ammo feeds the hunt. Parts fix the wagon. Medicine fixes people. Run out of any one of them and the trail starts taking things from you.

💀 Death Is Real

People get sick, break bones, drown, and starve. You can finish this game with two survivors, or none. Everyone who makes it earns you points.
